Services

Creative Planning

Our team is prepared to engage in extensive research and development, collect valuable market insights, and predict forthcoming trends. Collaboratively, we aim to design an effective brand strategy and cultivate a distinctive brand identity. Our goal is to amplify the prominence of your brand with utmost professionalism.

Product Design & Development

Line Apparel prioritizes product excellence, using our expertise in trend identification, fabric nuances, sourcing partnerships, and fit technology. We ensure a smooth process from concept to sale, creating empowering and comfortable apparel for our customers of all ages that enables style expression.

3D Technology

Leverage 3D technology to enhance product development and customer experience with pre-purchase virtual fit visualization. Simplify merchandising with advanced digital tools, and seamlessly manage media via our comprehensive digital libraries. Explore retail's future through our innovative digital solutions.

Production

We offer a comprehensive suite of services to ensure your global production sourcing is not only efficient but also socially and environmentally compliant. Our expertise extends to auditing and managing these compliance requirements, ensuring a smooth production process. Additionally, our on-ground quality control within the production country provides an added layer of assurance for on-time shipping.
